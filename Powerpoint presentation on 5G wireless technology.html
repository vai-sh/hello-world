Powerpoint presentation on 5G wireless technology

  1. 1. SHEKHAWATI INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY A SEMINAR PRESENTATION ON 5G WIRELESS TECHNOLOGY Submitted to:- Mrs. Suman Singh (Head Of Dept). Submitted by:- ParasNath Vishwkarma B.Tech CSE 8th sem
  2. 2. Contents…  Introduction to 5G  Evolution from 1G to 5G  Architecture  Hardware & Software of 5G  Features  Advantages  Applications  Conclusion
  3. 3. What is 5G?  5G Wireless:- 5th generation wireless technology. Complete wireless communication with almost no limitations. It is incredible transmission speed. Concept is only theory not real.
  4. 4. Evolution from 1G to 5G 1G 2G 3G 4G 5G
  5. 5. 1G Wireless Technology Developed in 1980s & completed in early 1990s. Allows user to make voice calls in 1 country. Based on analog system. Speed up to 2.4 kbps.
  6. 6. 2G Wireless Technology Developed in late 1980s & completed in late 1990s. Services such are digital voice & SMS with more clarity Based on digital system. Speed up to 64 kbps.
  7. 7. 3G Wireless Technology Developed between late 1990s & early 2000s. Transmission speed from 125 kbps to 2 Mbps. Good clarity in video conference. Use E-mail, shopping & banking.
  8. 8. 4G Wireless Technology Developed in 2010. Faster & more reliable. Speed up to 100 Mbps. High performance.
  9. 9. 5G Wireless Technology Next major phase of mobile telecommunication & wireless system. 10 times more capacity than others. Expected speed up to 1 Gbps. More faster & reliable than 4G.
  10. 10. Basic Architecture of 5G
  11. 11. 5G Hardware:- • Uses UWB (Ultra Wide Band) networks with higher Bandwidth at low energy levels. • Bandwidth is of 4000 Mbps, which is 400 times faster than today’s wireless networks. • Uses smart antenna. • Uses CDMA (Code Division Multiple Access).
  12. 12. 5G Software:- • 5G will be single unified standard of different wireless networks. • including LAN/WAN technologies, and WWWW- World Wide Wireless Web. • unified IP & seamless combination of broadband. • Software defined radio, encryption, flexibility, Anti-Virus.
  13. 13. Features of 5G:- • High resolution for crazy cell phone users. • More attractive & effective. • 25 Mbps connectivity speed. • Uploading & Downloading speed of 5G touching the peak (up to 1 Gbps). • Better & fast solution.
  14. 14. Applications of 5G:- Wearable devices with AI (Artificial Intelligence) capabilities. Media independent handover. Radio resource management. With 6th sense technology.
  15. 15. Conclusion 3G- Operator Centric,4G- Service Centric whereas 5G- User Centric We have proposed 5G wireless concept designed as an open platform on different layers The new coming 5G technology will be available in the market at affordable rates.
